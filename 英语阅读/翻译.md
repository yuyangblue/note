## 1
“一带”，指的是“丝绸之路经济带”（the Silk Road Economic Belt），是在陆地上。它有三大走向：从中国出发，一是经中亚、俄罗斯到达欧洲；二是经中亚、西亚至波斯湾（the Persian Gulf）、地中海（the Mediterranean）；三是从中国到东南亚、南亚、印度洋。


“一路”，指的是“21世纪海上丝绸之路”（the 21st-Century Maritime Silk Road），重点方向是两条：一是从中国沿海港口过南海（the South China Sea）到印度洋，延伸至欧洲；二是从中国沿海港口过南海到南太平洋。

“一带一路”（the Belt and Road）沿线国家大多属于发展中国家和转型经济体（transitional economy），经济发展后发优势强劲，与中国经济具有良好的互补性。
The “Belt” refers to the “Silk Road Economic Belt”, which is on land. It has three directions, starting from China. One goes through Central Asia and Russia to Europe; another passes through Central Asia, West Asia to the Persian Gulf and the Mediterranean; the third one connects China with Southeast Asia, South Asia and the Indian Ocean. The “Road” refers to the “21st-Century Maritime Silk Road” with two key directions: one is from China’s coastal ports across the South China Sea to the Indian Ocean, reaching Europe; the other is from China’s coastal ports across the South China Sea to the South Pacific. Most of the countries along the “Belt and Road” are developing countries or transitional economies. They have strong late-mover advantages in economic development and good complementarity with China’s economy.

## 2
一家人热热闹闹的年夜饭，边点评边看完的春晚（CCTV New Year’s Gala），喧嚣而缤纷的烟花鞭炮······年复一年，这些固定程序构成了我们对于春节的记忆，似乎有了这些仪式，才算过了一个完整的春节。

这些我们习以为常的仪式，其实都有着千年的文化根源。不过在时代的变迁中，许多习俗不论从形式还是内核，都发生了巨大的变化。

更有很多曾风靡一时的传统习俗，如今已经销声匿迹。
The lively New Year’s Eve family feast, the critical comments on the CCTV New Year’s Gala, the noisy and colorful firecrackers and fireworks ... these fixed procedures, year after year, form our memories of the Spring Festival. It seems that the festival is not complete without these rituals, which are taken for granted but have the cultural roots dating back thousands of years. However, many of these rituals have changed dramatically over the years, both in form and in essence. There are many traditional customs that were once popular but have now disappeared without a trace.

## 3
梁思成先生说：“观察西方传统建筑，我们就好像在看一幅西方的油画，可以从某一个确定的视角来完整地观察这座建筑，但是我们观察中国传统建筑就很不一样，看中国建筑就像看一幅卷轴画，我们打开画面是慢慢展开的，一点一点地才能完整地把这幅画所表达的空间感觉出来。”
Mr. Liang Sicheng said, “Observing Western traditional architecture is like appreciating a Western oil painting. We can fully observe the building from a certain perspective. However, when we observe traditional Chinese architecture, it is quite different. Observing Chinese architecture is similar to appreciating a scroll painting. We unfold the scroll slowly and can only appreciate it bit by bit to fully grasp the spatial feeling conveyed by the painting.”

## 4
踏着斑驳的青石板路，穿过幽深的小巷，推开厚重的木门，走进幽静的民居。经过门厅，首先映入眼帘的是一个天井。

抬头望去，蓝蓝的天空仿佛触手可及，给这幽暗的居所增添了一抹明亮的色彩。在下雨天，天上落下的雨水会顺着四周的屋顶注入天井，这就是俗称的“四水归明堂”。
Stepping on the mottled bluestone road, one walks into the deep alley, pushes open the heavy wooden door and then enters the quiet folk house. The first thing one can see is a patio after passing through the hall. Looking up, one could see the blue sky as if it could be touched, adding a bright color to the dark residence. On rainy days, the rain falling from the sky flows into the patio along the surrounding roofs, which is commonly known as the “water from all directions returning to the bright hall”.

## 5
在中国，和许多亚洲国家一样，家庭中的年长者因其智慧而受到尊重。儒家思想里“孝敬长辈”的概念深入人心。家庭中的长辈被其余的成员尊重和照顾，这一点无论是在过去还是现在都是每个家庭的原则。

在农村地区，许多家庭都是四五代人住在一起。在城市中，许多老人和子女住在一起，成为养育孙辈的主要角色。
As in many Asian cultures, in China the elders of the family are revered for their wisdom. The concept of filial piety in Confucianism is deep-rooted in people’s mind. Elders are respected and taken care of by the rest of the family, which was and is the principle of every household. In a rural environment, many households include four or five generations living together. In urban households, many elderly people live with their children. They typically play a large role in the raising of their grandchildren.
