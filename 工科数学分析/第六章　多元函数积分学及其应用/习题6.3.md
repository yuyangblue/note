## (A) 组

### 第 4 题　计算下列三重积分

#### (1) $\displaystyle\iiint_{(V)} e^{z}\,\mathrm dV$，$(V)$ 由 $x=0,\ y=1,\ z=0,\ y=x,\ z=x+y$ 围成

投影到 $xOy$ 面：$0\le x\le y\le 1$。

$$
I=\int_0^1\!\mathrm dy\int_0^{y}\!\mathrm dx\int_0^{x+y}\! e^{z}\,\mathrm dz
=\int_0^1\!\mathrm dy\int_0^{y}\!(e^{x+y}-1)\mathrm dx
=\int_0^1\!(e^{2y}-e^{y}-y)\mathrm dy=\boxed{\dfrac{e^{2}}{2}-e}.
$$

#### (2) $\displaystyle\iiint_{(V)} y\cos(x+z)\,\mathrm dV$，$(V)$：$y=\sqrt x,\ y=0,\ z=0,\ x+z=\tfrac{\pi}{2}$

$$
I=\int_0^{\pi/2}\!\mathrm dx\int_0^{\sqrt x}\! y\,\mathrm dy\int_0^{\pi/2-x}\!\cos(x+z)\mathrm dz
=\int_0^{\pi/2}\!\tfrac{x}{2}(1-\sin x)\mathrm dx=\boxed{\dfrac{\pi^{2}}{16}-\dfrac12}.
$$

#### (3) $\displaystyle\iiint_{(V)}\dfrac{e^{z}}{\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}}}\,\mathrm dV$，$(V)$：$z=\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}},\ z=1,\ z=2$

柱坐标：$1\le z\le 2,\ 0\le r\le z$。

$$
I=2\pi\!\int_1^{2}\!ze^{z}\mathrm dz=2\pi\bigl[(z-1)e^{z}\bigr]_1^{2}=\boxed{2\pi e^{2}}.
$$

#### (4) $\displaystyle\iiint_{(V)}(x^{2}+y^{2})\,\mathrm dV$，$(V)$：$x^{2}+y^{2}=2z,\ z=2$

柱坐标：$0\le r\le 2,\ r^{2}/2\le z\le 2$。

$$
I=2\pi\!\int_0^{2}\! r^{3}\!\left(2-\tfrac{r^{2}}{2}\right)\mathrm dr=\boxed{\dfrac{16\pi}{3}}.
$$

#### (5) $\displaystyle\iiint_{(V)} xy\,\mathrm dV$，$(V)$：$x^{2}+y^{2}\le1,\ 0\le z\le 1$，第一卦限

$$
I=\!\int_0^{\pi/2}\!\sin\theta\cos\theta\,\mathrm d\theta\!\int_0^{1}\! r^{3}\mathrm dr\!\int_0^{1}\!\mathrm dz=\tfrac12\cdot\tfrac14\cdot1=\boxed{\dfrac{1}{8}}.
$$

#### (6) $\displaystyle\iiint_{(V)} xy\,\mathrm dV$，$(V)$：$0\le z\le xy,\ x+y\le 1,\ x,y\ge 0$

$$
I=\!\int_0^{1}\!\mathrm dx\!\int_0^{1-x}\! x^{2}y^{2}\mathrm dy
=\!\int_0^{1}\!\tfrac{x^{2}(1-x)^{3}}{3}\mathrm dx=\tfrac13B(3,4)=\boxed{\dfrac{1}{180}}.
$$

#### (7) $\displaystyle\iiint_{(V)}(x^{2}+y^{2})\,\mathrm dV$，$(V)$：上半球壳 $a\le\rho\le A$

球坐标：

$$
I=2\pi\!\int_0^{\pi/2}\!\sin^{3}\!\varphi\,\mathrm d\varphi\!\int_a^{A}\!\rho^{4}\mathrm d\rho
=2\pi\cdot\tfrac{2}{3}\cdot\tfrac{A^{5}-a^{5}}{5}=\boxed{\dfrac{4\pi(A^{5}-a^{5})}{15}}.
$$

#### (8) $\displaystyle\iiint_{(V)} z\,\mathrm dV$，$(V)$：$z=\sqrt{4-x^{2}-y^{2}}$ 与 $z=\tfrac{1}{3}(x^{2}+y^{2})$

交线 $\sqrt{4-r^{2}}=\tfrac{r^{2}}{3}\Rightarrow r=\sqrt3$。

$$
I=\pi\!\int_0^{\sqrt3}\!\!\left[(4-r^{2})r-\tfrac{r^{5}}{9}\right]\!\mathrm dr=\pi\!\left(6-\tfrac{9}{4}-\tfrac12\right)=\boxed{\dfrac{13\pi}{4}}.
$$

#### (9) $\displaystyle\iiint_{(V)}\dfrac{\mathrm dV}{1+x^{2}+y^{2}}$，$(V)$：$x^{2}+y^{2}=z^{2},\ z=1$

柱坐标：$0\le r\le z\le 1$。

$$
I=2\pi\!\int_0^{1}\!\dfrac{r(1-r)}{1+r^{2}}\mathrm dr=\boxed{\pi\ln 2-2\pi+\dfrac{\pi^{2}}{2}}.
$$

#### (10) $\displaystyle\iiint_{(V)} z^{2}\,\mathrm dV$，$(V)$：$\rho\le R$ 与 $\rho\le 2R\cos\varphi$ 公共部分

临界 $\varphi=\pi/3$：

$$
I=2\pi\!\left\{\tfrac{R^{5}}{5}\!\int_0^{\pi/3}\!\cos^{2}\!\varphi\sin\varphi\,\mathrm d\varphi+\tfrac{32R^{5}}{5}\!\int_{\pi/3}^{\pi/2}\!\cos^{7}\!\varphi\sin\varphi\,\mathrm d\varphi\right\}
$$

$$
=2\pi R^{5}\!\left(\tfrac{7}{120}+\tfrac{1}{320}\right)=\boxed{\dfrac{59\pi R^{5}}{480}}.
$$

#### (11) $\displaystyle\iiint_{(V)} xyz\,\mathrm dV$，$(V)$：单位球第一卦限

$$
I=\!\int_0^{\pi/2}\!\sin\theta\cos\theta\,\mathrm d\theta\!\int_0^{\pi/2}\!\sin^{3}\!\varphi\cos\varphi\,\mathrm d\varphi\!\int_0^{1}\!\rho^{5}\mathrm d\rho=\tfrac12\cdot\tfrac14\cdot\tfrac16=\boxed{\dfrac{1}{48}}.
$$

#### (12) $\displaystyle\iiint_{(V)}\sqrt{1-\rho^{2}}\,\mathrm dV$，$(V)$：$\rho\le1,\ z\ge\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}}$

$$
I=2\pi\!\left(1-\tfrac{\sqrt2}{2}\right)\!\int_0^{1}\!\rho^{2}\sqrt{1-\rho^{2}}\,\mathrm d\rho
=2\pi\!\left(1-\tfrac{\sqrt2}{2}\right)\cdot\tfrac{\pi}{16}=\boxed{\dfrac{\pi^{2}(2-\sqrt2)}{16}}.
$$

#### (13) $\displaystyle\iiint_{(V)}(x+y)\,\mathrm dV$，$(V)$：$1\le x^{2}+y^{2}\le 4,\ 0\le z\le x+2$

$$
I=\int_0^{2\pi}\!\mathrm d\theta\!\int_1^{2}\!(r\cos\theta+r\sin\theta)(r\cos\theta+2)r\,\mathrm dr
=\pi\!\int_1^{2}\! r^{3}\mathrm dr=\boxed{\dfrac{15\pi}{4}}.
$$

#### (14) $\displaystyle\iiint_{(V)}\dfrac{z\ln(1+x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2})}{1+x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}}\mathrm dV$，$(V)$：单位球

被积函数关于 $z$ 奇，区域对称：$I=\boxed{0}$。

#### (15) $\displaystyle\iiint_{(V)} z(x^{2}+y^{2})\mathrm dV$，$(V)$：$z\ge\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}},\ 1\le\rho\le 2$

$$
I=2\pi\!\int_0^{\pi/4}\!\sin^{3}\!\varphi\cos\varphi\,\mathrm d\varphi\!\int_1^{2}\!\rho^{5}\mathrm d\rho=2\pi\cdot\tfrac{1}{16}\cdot\tfrac{63}{6}=\boxed{\dfrac{21\pi}{16}}.
$$

#### (16) $\displaystyle\iiint_{(V)} z\,\mathrm dV$，$(V)$：$x^{2}+y^{2}+(z-a)^{2}\le a^{2}$ 与锥 $x^{2}+y^{2}\le z^{2}$

球坐标：$\rho\le 2a\cos\varphi,\ \varphi\le\pi/4$。

$$
I=8\pi a^{4}\!\int_0^{\pi/4}\!\sin\varphi\cos^{5}\!\varphi\,\mathrm d\varphi=8\pi a^{4}\cdot\tfrac{7/8}{6}=\boxed{\dfrac{7\pi a^{4}}{6}}.
$$

### 第 5 题　选用合适坐标系计算累次积分

#### (1) $\displaystyle\int_{-1}^{1}\!\mathrm dx\!\int_{0}^{\sqrt{1-x^{2}}}\!\mathrm dy\!\int_{\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}}}^{1}\!z^{2}\mathrm dz$

柱坐标：$0\le\theta\le\pi$。

$$
I=\pi\!\int_0^{1}\! r\,\mathrm dr\!\int_r^{1}\!z^{2}\mathrm dz=\tfrac{\pi}{3}\!\int_0^{1}\! r(1-r^{3})\mathrm dr=\boxed{\dfrac{\pi}{10}}.
$$

#### (2) $\displaystyle\int_{-3}^{3}\!\mathrm dx\!\int_{-\sqrt{9-x^{2}}}^{\sqrt{9-x^{2}}}\!\mathrm dy\!\int_{0}^{\sqrt{9-x^{2}-y^{2}}}\! z\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}}\mathrm dz$

上半球，球坐标：

$$
I=2\pi\!\int_0^{\pi/2}\!\sin\varphi\cos\varphi\,\mathrm d\varphi\!\int_0^{3}\!\rho^{4}\mathrm d\rho=2\pi\cdot\tfrac12\cdot\tfrac{243}{5}=\boxed{\dfrac{243\pi}{5}}.
$$

### 第 6 题　立体体积

#### (1) $\rho=a,\ \rho=b$ 与上半锥 $z=\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}}$

$$
V=2\pi\!\left(1-\tfrac{\sqrt2}{2}\right)\!\cdot\tfrac{b^{3}-a^{3}}{3}=\boxed{\dfrac{(2-\sqrt2)\pi(b^{3}-a^{3})}{3}}.
$$

#### (2) $z=6-x^{2}-y^{2}$ 与 $z=\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}}$

交线 $r=2$。

$$
V=2\pi\!\int_0^{2}\! r(6-r^{2}-r)\mathrm dr=\boxed{\dfrac{32\pi}{3}}.
$$

#### (3) $(x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2})^{2}=a^{3}z$

球坐标 $\rho=a\sqrt[3]{\cos\varphi},\ 0\le\varphi\le\pi/2$：

$$
V=\tfrac{2\pi a^{3}}{3}\!\int_0^{\pi/2}\!\sin\varphi\cos\varphi\,\mathrm d\varphi=\boxed{\dfrac{\pi a^{3}}{3}}.
$$

#### (4) $x=\sqrt{y-z^{2}},\ x=\tfrac12\sqrt y,\ y=1$

$$
V=2\!\int_0^{1}\!\mathrm dy\!\int_0^{\sqrt y/2}\!\sqrt{y-x^{2}}\,\mathrm dx
=2\!\int_0^1\! y\!\left(\tfrac{\pi}{12}+\tfrac{\sqrt3}{8}\right)\!\mathrm dy=\boxed{\dfrac{\pi}{12}+\dfrac{\sqrt3}{8}}.
$$

### 第 7 题　

$y^{2}=2z$（$x=0$）绕 $z$ 轴旋转得 $x^{2}+y^{2}=2z$，与 $z=8$ 所围立体。

$$
I=\!\iiint_{(V)}(x^{2}+y^{2})\mathrm dV=2\pi\!\int_0^{4}\! r^{3}\!\left(8-\tfrac{r^{2}}{2}\right)\mathrm dr=\boxed{\dfrac{1024\pi}{3}}.
$$

### 第 8 题　

抛物面 $z=x^{2}+y^{2}+1$ 在点 $(a,b,a^{2}+b^{2}+1)$ 的切平面为

$$
z=2ax+2by-a^{2}-b^{2}+1.
$$

切平面与 $z=x^{2}+y^{2}$ 之差 $=1-(x-a)^{2}-(y-b)^{2}$。令 $u=x-a,v=y-b$：

$$
V=\!\iint_{u^{2}+v^{2}\le1}\!(1-u^{2}-v^{2})\mathrm du\mathrm dv=2\pi\!\int_0^1\!(1-r^{2})r\,\mathrm dr=\boxed{\dfrac{\pi}{2}}.
$$

结果与切点位置无关，故恒为常数。$\blacksquare$

## (B) 组

### 第 1 题

#### ① $\displaystyle\iiint_{(V)}\dfrac{\mathrm dV}{\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}}}$，$(V)$：$\rho\le 2\cos\varphi,\ z\ge 1,\ y\ge 0$

以原点为极的球坐标：$\sec\varphi\le\rho\le2\cos\varphi,\ \varphi\le\pi/4,\ 0\le\theta\le\pi$。

$$
I=\tfrac{\pi}{2}\!\int_0^{\pi/4}\!\sin\varphi(4\cos^{2}\!\varphi-\sec^{2}\!\varphi)\mathrm d\varphi
=\tfrac{\pi}{2}\!\left[\tfrac{4-\sqrt2}{3}-(\sqrt2-1)\right]=\boxed{\dfrac{\pi(7-4\sqrt2)}{6}}.
$$

#### ② $\displaystyle\iiint_{(V)}\bigl|\rho-1\bigr|\,\mathrm dV$，$(V)$：$z=\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}},\ z=1$

球坐标：$0\le\varphi\le\pi/4,\ 0\le\rho\le\sec\varphi$。

$$
\int_0^{\sec\varphi}\!|\rho-1|\rho^{2}\mathrm d\rho=\tfrac16+\tfrac{\sec^{4}\!\varphi}{4}-\tfrac{\sec^{3}\!\varphi}{3}.
$$

利用 $\int_0^{\pi/4}\!\sin\varphi/\cos^{4}\!\varphi\,\mathrm d\varphi=\tfrac{2\sqrt2-1}{3}$，$\int_0^{\pi/4}\!\sin\varphi/\cos^{3}\!\varphi\,\mathrm d\varphi=\tfrac12$：

$$
I=2\pi\cdot\dfrac{\sqrt2-1}{12}=\boxed{\dfrac{\pi(\sqrt2-1)}{6}}.
$$

#### ③ $\displaystyle\iiint_{(V)}\sqrt{1-\tfrac{x^{2}}{a^{2}}-\tfrac{y^{2}}{b^{2}}-\tfrac{z^{2}}{c^{2}}}\,\mathrm dV$，$(V)$：椭球

广义球坐标，$|J|=abc\rho^{2}\sin\varphi$：

$$
I=abc\cdot 2\pi\cdot 2\!\int_0^{1}\!\rho^{2}\sqrt{1-\rho^{2}}\mathrm d\rho=4\pi abc\cdot\tfrac{\pi}{16}=\boxed{\dfrac{\pi^{2}abc}{4}}.
$$

### 第 3 题

$F(t)=\!\iiint_{(V)} x\ln(1+x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2})\mathrm dV$，$(V)$：$\rho\le t,\ \sqrt{y^{2}+z^{2}}\le x$。

以 $x$ 轴为极轴的球坐标：$\varphi\le\pi/4$。

$$
F(t)=\tfrac{\pi}{2}\!\int_0^{t}\!\rho^{3}\ln(1+\rho^{2})\mathrm d\rho
\Rightarrow\boxed{F'(t)=\dfrac{\pi}{2}\,t^{3}\ln(1+t^{2})}.
$$

$F(t)=\!\iiint_{\rho\le t}\!f(\rho^{2})\mathrm dV=4\pi\!\int_0^{t}\!\rho^{2}f(\rho^{2})\mathrm d\rho$

$$
\boxed{F'(t)=4\pi t^{2}f(t^{2})}.

### 第 5 题

$(V)=\{0\le z\le h,\ x^{2}+y^{2}\le t^{2}\}$，$F(t)=\!\iiint_{(V)}\![z^{2}+f(x^{2}+y^{2})]\mathrm dV$。

$$
F(t)=\tfrac{\pi h^{3}t^{2}}{3}+2\pi h\!\int_0^{t}\! r f(r^{2})\mathrm dr.
$$

$$
\boxed{\dfrac{\mathrm dF}{\mathrm dt}=\dfrac{2\pi h^{3}t}{3}+2\pi h\,t\,f(t^{2})},\quad
\boxed{\lim_{t\to 0}\dfrac{F(t)}{t^{2}}=\dfrac{\pi h^{3}}{3}+\pi h f(0)}.
$$

### 第 6 题

$\iiint_{(V)}(x+y+z)^{2}\mathrm dV$，$(V)$：椭球 $\tfrac{x^{2}}{a^{2}}+\tfrac{y^{2}}{b^{2}}+\tfrac{z^{2}}{c^{2}}\le1$。

由对称性，交叉项 $xy,yz,zx$ 积分为零，仅剩平方项。利用广义球坐标：

$$
\iiint x^{2}\mathrm dV=\tfrac{4\pi a^{3}bc}{15},\ \iiint y^{2}\mathrm dV=\tfrac{4\pi ab^{3}c}{15},\ \iiint z^{2}\mathrm dV=\tfrac{4\pi abc^{3}}{15}.
$$

$$
\boxed{I=\dfrac{4\pi abc(a^{2}+b^{2}+c^{2})}{15}}.
$$
