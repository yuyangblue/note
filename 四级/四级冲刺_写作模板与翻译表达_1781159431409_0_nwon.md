### 模板1：议论文（最常见）

**题目类型**：对某现象/观点发表看法

> **第一段（引出话题+亮明观点，约40词）**
>
> Nowadays, there is a growing concern over 话题. Some people argue that 观点A, while others believe that 观点B. As far as I am concerned, I firmly support 你的观点.
>
> **第二段（论证，约60词）**
>
> To begin with, 理由1. For example, 具体例子. In addition, 理由2. A case in point is that 具体例子. Therefore, it is evident that 重申观点.
>
> **第三段（总结，约30词）**
>
> In conclusion, taking all these factors into consideration, I strongly believe that 重申观点. Only in this way can we 展望.

**示例：Should college students take part-time jobs?**

> Nowadays, there is a growing concern over whether college students should take part-time jobs. Some people argue that it distracts students from their studies, while others believe it provides valuable experience. As far as I am concerned, I firmly support that students should take part-time jobs under proper conditions.
>
> To begin with, part-time jobs help students gain practical experience. For example, working as a tutor can improve one's communication skills and deepen their understanding of academic knowledge. In addition, earning money by themselves cultivates a sense of responsibility. A case in point is that many students who support themselves become more independent and confident. Therefore, it is evident that part-time jobs benefit students in multiple ways.
>
> In conclusion, taking all these factors into consideration, I strongly believe that college students should be encouraged to take part-time jobs. Only in this way can they better prepare themselves for future careers.
